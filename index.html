What is the unseen in the world filled with technology and our devices glued to our hands?







It's easy to get caught in what we see on our phones and in the midst of chaos when work and life gets busy... we turn to technology for temporary satisfaction and relief, that we miss out on the little things in life.

Maybe we're escaping from reality?

Sometimes when we're too focused on our devices, and we forget to live in the moment, the key to life, is just to shutdown the device and look at the things in front of us. Those are what matter's the most, because what we do now, is a part of a memory we'll carry on with us forever in the future.

– Alexis Trent
